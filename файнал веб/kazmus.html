Famous Kazakh performers

Moldanazar (Galymzhan Moldanazar)

At the time of my last update in January 2022, the Moldanazar artist showed his contribution to the Kazakh language through music, lyrics and his activity in the field of musical culture. He used the Kazakh language in his songs, which contributes to the popularization of the language among the younger generation.

Miras Zhagunusov

Miras Zhugunusov has made his contribution to the Kazakh language and culture by singing songs in both Kazakh and Russian. His musical style combines various genres, such as pop, R&B and soul, which allows his compositions to be accessible and understandable to a wide audience.

NINETY ONE

Ninety One have had a significant impact on young people, drawing attention to the culture and language of their country through their music and style. They have become a symbol of the modern Kazakh music industry and demonstrated that the Kazakh language can be successfully used in popular music and find its audience not only locally but also globally.
