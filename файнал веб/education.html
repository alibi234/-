Soyle.kz

One of the largest projects for the study of the Kazakh language. The portal consists of six sections, and the training course itself is divided into 6 levels. Each of them consists of 10 lessons.

Tilqural.kz

Web platform for self-study of the Kazakh language. The content was created by Kazakh linguists, and the training includes interactive exercises with games.

Tilmedia.kz

The site makes it possible to learn the Kazakh language by reading, watching and listening — the teaching methodology on the site is based on audio and video. Video tutorials, cartoons, audiobooks, textbooks.

Kitap.kz

Learning a language from books is one of the most effective ways of learning. No other language exercise allows you to expand your vocabulary so significantly. The library of the site includes more than 5000 books.

Ana tili

Learning Kazakh with Ana tili is like going through a quest. The application awards points for solving problems. In it, you can collect sentences, guess words, write dictation and train in Latin.

Qazaq

The application helps to learn the Kazakh language using modern technologies and specially developed methodology. In it you can replenish your vocabulary and learn facts about the cities of Kazakhstan.

Beginner Kazakh

The application is designed for English-speaking users and works according to the concept of "words of the day". You can configure the app so that it outputs up to 12 new words daily.

Kazbooka

Youtube is a channel for learning the Kazakh language for the Russian-speaking audience. The author of the channel Elena Romanenko is the author of her own methodology of teaching the Kazakh language to Russian speakers.

BilimKids

Children's Youtube channel with songs in Kazakh with bright pictures and catchy melodies. Five-minute videos with text accompaniment, like in karaoke, help you better remember the spelling of words.

Soyletube

The channel consists of video tutorials aimed at improving the pronunciation of the Kazakh language. The host of the channel is a well—known blogger Akynych in Kazakhstan

Maksat Imangazi

You haven't practiced English for a long time and want to learn Kazakh — the author's channel of Maksat Imangazy is a great opportunity to "pump" two languages at once.

Aitube.kz

Learning Kazakh from films and TV series is not only interesting, but also extremely useful. Aitube is a national media hosting that combines exclusive content from professional video makers and users.
